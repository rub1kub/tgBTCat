privacy policy

the tgBTCat site does not ask for accounts, passwords, seed phrases, or private keys.

when you connect a wallet, the interface receives the public wallet address and uses it to find token balances and prepare transactions.

public blockchain transactions, votes, addresses, and balances are visible on TON and may be indexed by explorers and other third-party services.

basic server logs may include request time, IP address, browser information, and requested URLs for security and operational diagnostics.
